Mayones Regius Pro 8

When even 7 strings are not enough to set your creativity loose from the depth of mind, a Regius 8 is the solution. Whether you seek ambient clean passages high up the strings or djent power from down under Regius 8 lets you express what you hide deep inside.

A Regius 8 sports a profiled Flamed Maple top on a Swamp Ash or optional Mahogany body and its highly recognizable 11-ply neck-thru laminate neck with baritone (687mm) scale.

Comes with EMG 808 or Seymour Duncan Blackouts 8 pickups, Sperzel Trimlock tuners, ABM fixed bridge with strings-thru-body and Schaller Security Straplocks.

Customer choice pickups available on demand at extra fee.

Dedicated hard case included.
